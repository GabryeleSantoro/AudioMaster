AudioMaster

The free audio control center for macOS.

Download for Mac

Requires macOS 14.2+

What it does

Control your Mac’s audio from one place — apps, devices, and EQ.

  • Per-app volume

    Set volume or mute for each app on its own.

  • Equalizer

    Shape sound globally or per app with built-in presets.

  • Device switching

    See outputs and inputs and switch without System Settings.

  • Bluetooth battery

    Live battery levels on wireless audio gear.

  • Menu bar control

    Quick volumes and devices from the menu bar.

  • Routing presets

    Save a full setup and restore it in one click.
